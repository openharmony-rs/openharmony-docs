## Overview

Provides the capabilities of creating a color space and obtaining color space attributes. It is applicable to scenarios that require precise color management and conversion, such as image processing and screen display. With this module, you can create criterion color spaces (such as sRGB and Display P3), obtain color space attributes, and solve problems such as cross-device color consistency and color precision control. This ensures that your app presents consistent color effects on different devices.

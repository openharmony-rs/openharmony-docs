**继承/实现关系：** GaugeConfiguration extends [CommonConfiguration<GaugeConfiguration>](CommonConfiguration<GaugeConfiguration>)

**起始版本：** 12

**系统能力：** SystemCapability.ArkUI.ArkUI.Full
